The Akan Name Generator!

With this application,you will enter your birthday (day of the month,month and year) and your gender then it will calculates the day of the week you were born and then depending on your gender ,it will output your Akan Name. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names.

Male

  1. Sunday: Kwasi
  2. Monday: Kwadwo
  3. Tuesday: Kwabena
  4. Wednesday: Kwaku
  5. Thursday: Yaw
  6. Friday: Kofi
  7. Saturday: Kwame

Female

  1. Sunday: Akosua
  2. Monday: Adwoa
  3. Tuesday: Abenaa
  4. Wednesday: Akua
  5. Thursday: Yaa
  6. Friday: Afua
  7. Saturday: Ama
